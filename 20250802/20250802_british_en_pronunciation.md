# Pronunciation Guide for AI Product Exhibition Speech

I'm going to talk about an AI product exhibition.
/aɪm ˈɡəʊɪŋ tə tɔːk əˈbaʊt ən eɪˈaɪ ˈprɒdʌkt ˌeksɪˈbɪʃən/

Last week, I attended an AI product exhibition.
/lɑːst wiːk aɪ əˈtendɪd ən eɪˈaɪ ˈprɒdʌkt ˌeksɪˈbɪʃən/

I'm also developing products that integrate AI, so I was really curious about what others were creating.
/aɪm ˈɔːlsəʊ dɪˈveləpɪŋ ˈprɒdʌkts ðæt ˈɪntɪɡreɪt eɪˈaɪ səʊ aɪ wəz ˈrɪəli ˈkjʊəriəs əˈbaʊt wɒt ˈʌðəz wɜː kriˈeɪtɪŋ/

Some companies were exhibiting, but the main exhibitors were individual developers.
/sʌm ˈkʌmpəniz wɜːr ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪŋ bʌt ðə meɪn ɪɡˈzɪbɪtəz wɜːr ˌɪndɪˈvɪdjuəl dɪˈveləpəz/

All the exhibitors were really passionate, and their products were carefully crafted down to the smallest details and had unique features.
/ɔːl ðə ɪɡˈzɪbɪtəz wɜː ˈrɪəli ˈpæʃənət ænd ðeə ˈprɒdʌkts wɜː ˈkeəfəli ˈkrɑːftɪd daʊn tə ðə ˈsmɔːlɪst ˈdiːteɪlz ænd hæd juːˈniːk ˈfiːtʃəz/

Many of them explained in detail how things worked and why they built them.
/ˈmeni əv ðəm ɪksˈpleɪnd ɪn ˈdiːteɪl haʊ θɪŋz wɜːkt ænd waɪ ðeɪ bɪlt ðəm/

And most visitors were also individual developers, so every exhibition space was always bustling.
/ænd məʊst ˈvɪzɪtəz wɜːr ˈɔːlsəʊ ˌɪndɪˈvɪdjuəl dɪˈveləpəz səʊ ˈevri ˌeksɪˈbɪʃən speɪs wəz ˈɔːlweɪz ˈbʌslɪŋ/

The exhibition was really interesting.
/ðə ˌeksɪˈbɪʃən wəz ˈrɪəli ˈɪntrəstɪŋ/

In particular, I was really interested in a self-management app using an avatar and its developer.
/ɪn pəˈtɪkjələ aɪ wəz ˈrɪəli ˈɪntrəstɪd ɪn ə self ˈmænɪdʒmənt æp ˈjuːzɪŋ ən ˈævətɑː ænd ɪts dɪˈveləpə/

The developer told me he's really terrible at self-management, so he created this app.
/ðə dɪˈveləpə təʊld miː hiːz ˈrɪəli ˈterəbl æt self ˈmænɪdʒmənt səʊ hiː kriˈeɪtɪd ðɪs æp/

He said text notifications felt too bland, but with an avatar, it felt like a real person was pushing him to do things.
/hiː sed tekst ˌnəʊtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz felt tuː blænd bʌt wɪð ən ˈævətɑː ɪt felt laɪk ə rɪəl ˈpɜːsən wəz ˈpʊʃɪŋ hɪm tə duː θɪŋz/

This made it easier for him to take action.
/ðɪs meɪd ɪt ˈiːziə fɔː hɪm tə teɪk ˈækʃən/

I'd never thought of it that way, and the app used advanced and complex technology, so I found it really interesting.
/aɪd ˈnevə θɔːt əv ɪt ðæt weɪ ænd ðə æp juːzd ədˈvɑːnst ænd ˈkɒmpleks tekˈnɒlədʒi səʊ aɪ faʊnd ɪt ˈrɪəli ˈɪntrəstɪŋ/

Overall, the exhibition was both interesting and educational.
/ˈəʊvərɔːl ðə ˌeksɪˈbɪʃən wəz bəʊθ ˈɪntrəstɪŋ ænd ˌedjuˈkeɪʃənəl/

That's all, thank you.
/ðæts ɔːl θæŋk juː/
